About Us

Feed Forward is a non-profit initiative started by NSBM Group 06 with the mission to combat hunger and malnutrition in our community. Our innovative NutriBox program provides essential nutrition to those in need while promoting sustainable development.

We align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 2 (Zero Hunger) to ensure access to safe, nutritious and sufficient food for all people year-round.

Our Mission

To eradicate hunger in our community by providing nutritious food packages and promoting food security awareness.

Our Vision

A world where no one goes to bed hungry and everyone has access to basic nutrition.

The NutriBox Project

Each NutriBox contains essential food items carefully selected to provide balanced nutrition for a family of four for one week.

What's Inside

Rice, lentils, cooking oil, flour, sugar, tea, milk powder, canned fish, and fresh vegetables when available.

Who We Help

Low-income families, orphanages, elderly homes, and communities affected by natural disasters.

Our Progress

Since our launch, we've distributed over 500 NutriBoxes, feeding more than 2,000 people in need.
